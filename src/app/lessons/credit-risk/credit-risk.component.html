Credit Risk Meauring

What is Credit Risk and how do we measure it?

Credit Risk is the risk that a firm defaults on its loan (credit).

There are multiple credit rating systems including the S&P and Moddy's raiting system. Ratings typically follow a AAA, AA, A, BBB, BB, B, CCC, CC, and C rating style with AAA meaning that firm's debt has a low probability of defaulting. Bonds with ratings of BBB or higher are considered to be "investment grade".
