What is product management?

It includes:

  • Development
  • Sales
  • Legal
  • Finance
  • Research
  • Marketing
  • Support
  • Customers

A Product is a bundle of physical, service, and symbolic attributes designed to enhance buyers' want satisfaction

You can think of a product as a core benefit or service. Around that product are key features of that benefit/service such as:

  • Packaging
  • Styling
  • Features
  • Quality
  • Brand Name

These features are directly attributed to the product. Other attributes such as Installation, Delivery & Credit, After Sales Service, and Warranty can be thought of as after purchase experiences.
